# v0.2.0 — 2026-07-08

The "local guide" release: repositioned from civic-data lookup toward
what's-happening-around-here, with events as a first-class tool.

## Added

- `dfw_events`: official city calendars (Dallas **Parks & Recreation only** —
  no citywide Dallas feed exists, and the tool says so — plus Garland, Frisco,
  Mesquite, McKinney via CivicPlus RSS) and an optional Ticketmaster tier
  (`DFW_TICKETMASTER_API_KEY`, free) for concerts/sports/theater metro-wide.

## Why it's shaped this way

- Plano, Arlington, Fort Worth, and Irving publish no usable calendar feed;
  the tool returns "not covered" for them instead of guessing. Keyless
  installs get city calendars only, with a hint about the free key.
